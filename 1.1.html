What is Machine learning

Machine learnin is a growing technology which enables computers to learn
automatically from past data. Machine learning uses various algorithms for building
mathematical models and making predictions using historical data or information.
Currently, it is being used for various tasks such as image recognition, speech recognition, email filtering, Facebook auto-tagging, recommender system, and many more

In the real world, we are surrounded by humans who can learn everything from their experiences with their learning capability,
and we have computers or machines which work on our instructions.
But can a machine also learn from experiences or past data like a human does?
So here comes the role of Machine Learning.
